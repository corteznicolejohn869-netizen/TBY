Fabric for Your Tuxedo

Fabric selection is the heart of bespoke tailoring. Discover our premium materials.

Classic Tuxedo Fabrics

Merino Wool

Wool (The Most Common)

**Ideal:** All-year formal events. Excellent drape and breathable. It is the most versatile and classic choice.

  • Excellent draping and wrinkle-resistance.
  • Available in "Super 100s" up to "Super 180s" quality.
Mohair (For Sheen)

Mohair (For Sheen)

**Ideal:** Spring/Summer events. Provides a subtle sheen and is more durable. It feels lighter and crisper.

  • Naturally has a lustrous finish.
  • More durable and crease-resistant.
Silk / Satin

Silk or Satin (Accents)

**Ideal:** Lapel, buttons, and trouser stripe. Silk (or satin) provides a formal and strong contrast against matte wool.

  • Used for the lapel facings.
  • Provides elegant shine and texture.
Luxury Velvet

Velvet (Modern Look)

**Ideal:** Dinner jackets and semi-formal events. Provides a deep, rich, and dramatic texture. Popular for modern weddings.

  • Very luxurious and soft to the touch.
  • Typically used for the jacket only.

Fabric Weight and Climate

Fabric weight is important, especially in tropical climates like the Philippines. We ensure your chosen fabric is suitable for the temperature and occasion.

Lightweight

**E.g., 7oz - 9oz.** For tropical climates or summer weddings. Offers good air circulation but wrinkles more easily.

Mid-weight

**E.g., 10oz - 12oz.** This is the most versatile option. Provides good drape and durability. Suitable for air-conditioned venues.
