Viscose Swatch Preview

Evening Gown Fabric: Silk, Viscose, & Lycra Blend

For stunning, figure-flattering evening gowns, this premium blend combines the natural luster of silk, the fluid drape of viscose, and the essential comfort and stretch of Lycra. Perfect for bias-cut and fitted silhouettes that require movement and a beautiful sheen.

  • Fully customizable monograms
