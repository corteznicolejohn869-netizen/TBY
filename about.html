Our Heritage of Craftsmanship

Where Filipino tradition meets modern innovation to create the perfect fit.

The Genesis in Lucban, Quezon

Tailored By You was founded in 2010 by master tailors Manglicmot and Oblifeas, establishing their main atelier in **Lucban, Quezon**. They merged decades of classical European tailoring techniques with the rich textile heritage of the Philippines. Frustrated by the exclusive nature of traditional houses, they aimed to democratize bespoke tailoring, allowing customers worldwide to design, measure, and receive garments that fit perfectly, crafted with authentic Filipino care.

"We honor the local artistry of Quezon while embracing global innovation to deliver excellence." - Rezel Villalva.

Our Core Philosophy: Quality, Sustainability, and Fit

Exceptional Quality

We source fine fabrics globally and locally, specializing in natural, breathable materials often found in the best Philippine textiles.

Sustainable Craftsmanship

Bespoke means zero waste. We only cut fabric upon order, minimizing environmental impact and supporting ethical Filipino artisan communities.

The Perfect Fit

Our proprietary Analyzing measurement tools and experienced tailors guarantee a fit that is truly personalized, fused with the precision of Asian sizing standards.
